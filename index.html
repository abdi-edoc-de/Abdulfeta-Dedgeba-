I took my primary and secondary education at Nafyad School located at the center of Adama or
commonly known as Nazreth.Nafyad School is a branch of the well-known Rift Valley University.
Nafyadians are renowned for their amazing skills at playing soccer. It has won regional soccer
competitions several times, among which is last year’s championship. I am not a very good soccer
player but I can confidently say that I was one of the few students who achieved to integrate both
